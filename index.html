About Me

Hello! I'm Khayrullo Isomiddinov, born on December 28, 2003, in Namangan, Uzbekistan. My educational journey has been diverse, starting with my early years of schooling in Russia at School No. 117. Upon returning to my homeland, I continued my education in Uzbekistan, where I developed a passion for languages. During the latter part of my high school years, I delved into learning English and discovered a talent for teaching languages. For a year, I enjoyed a fulfilling career as a language instructor before pivoting to pursue my interest in computer science and programming. My dedication and enthusiasm for technology led me to win the prestigious Stipendium Hungaricum in 2022, granting me the opportunity to pursue my higher education in Budapest, where I currently reside. I am passionately engaged in studying computer science, where every line of code written is a step forward in my quest for knowledge and innovation. Join me in exploring the infinite possibilities of technology and its impact on the world!
